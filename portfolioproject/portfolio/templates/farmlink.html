An auto generated class schedule and management system. A system to minimize workload, avoid conflicts and organize room assignments in creating class schedules. With automation, the system turns days worth of work in less than an hour.



e-ClaSched was a capstone project we proposed and developed back in 2024. The purpose was to automate the creation of class schedules in Negros Oriental State University, Bayawan Santa Catalina Campus. With over 6 colleges and a hundred instructors, it makes sure that there are no conflicts between instructor's class schedules and fair distribution of time and assignment of rooms.


As the sole developer of e-ClaSched, I played a pivotal role in shaping the system's architecture, design and functionality. It was a challenge for me considering the complexity and wide scope of the project. e-clasched success was the result of hardwork, planning and problem solving. Now, e-Clasched has been implemented and now being used in the university.



Technologies used:

Python, Django, Bootstrap, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
