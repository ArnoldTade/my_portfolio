Farmlink


Farmlink One Stop Shop is an innovative e-commerce web application specifically designed to cater to the needs of farmers, offering a convenient online platform for purchasing a variety of agricultural products. The platform features a diverse range of products including vegetables, fruits, root crops, spices, and grains.



The development of Farmlink One Stop Shop originated from a feasibility study conducted within the field of business administration. The primary focus of this study was to evaluate the potential and practicality of such a platform for farmers, with a particular emphasis on the agricultural community in Bayawan City. The study aimed to understand the specific needs and challenges faced by these farmers and to develop a tailored solution that could enhance their access to essential agricultural products while also streamlining their purchasing process.



Technologies used:

Python, Django, Bootstrap, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
